Definition: School Grounds

Playing fields or ovals and sports courts or surfaces adjacent to and/or owned by a school or university. These may or may not be accessible for public use out of school hours. The area identified does not include the school buildings.

Source Orthoimagery: Western Australian Land Information Authority (Landgate), Aerial orthophotography 2012, Midland, Western Australia.
